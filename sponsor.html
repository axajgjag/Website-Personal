Bank Mandiri

Bank Mandiri was established in July 1999 as a result of the merger of four state-owned banks: Bank Bumi Daya, Bank Dagang Negara, Bank Exim, and Bapindo. As the largest bank in Indonesia, Bank Mandiri provides commercial, SME, and retail banking services, and supports national infrastructure and economic projects. The bank also focuses on digital transformation through services such as Mandiri Online and Mobile, as well as expanding internationally to support global businesses. Bank Mandiri continues to play an important role in Indonesia's economy with more than 140 years of heritage.

Ikatan Alumni Politeknik Batam

The Batam State Polytechnic Alumni Association (IA POLBAT) is an organisation that gathers Polibatam alumni to support the career and development of graduates. IA POLBAT assists alumni in finding jobs, provides financial assistance for students, and organises training and seminars to improve skills and work readiness.

PT Servotech Indonesia

PT Servotech Indonesia, established on 7 March 2003, specialises in automation and control for the oil and gas industry. Starting in Indonesia, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East, the company expanded its global market in 2006. Servotech products are trusted by oil companies in Indonesia and exported to Malaysia, Thailand, and Canada.

GEMPACS

GEMPACS is a company dedicated to revolutionizing marine mobility by converting professional fishing and water taxi boats to fully electric systems. They retrofit boats with electric motors, solar roofs, and batteries, significantly reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Operating primarily in Indonesia, GEMPACS installs charging stations at ports and uses IoT technology for efficient boat management. They also facilitate loans for boat owners to transition to electric systems without upfront costs, enhancing their income through lower operating expenses. GEMPACS plans to expand further into Southeast Asia and other markets.

Octagon

PT. Octagon Precision Indonesia, founded in September 2002, specializes in general fabrication, engineering machine parts, tooling, and oilfield mechanical equipment. Based in Batam, the company serves both multinational and local clients by providing generators, pumps, and utility equipment. The company is known for its commitment to quality and timely delivery, holding ISO 9001:2015 certification. They also offer construction services focused on electrical installations for power generation and renewable energy projects.
