About Us

Barelang MRT is a robotics team from Politeknik Negeri Batam specializing in surface, underwater, and aerial robotics. The team now consists of three divisions: Barelang MRT - Nereus: Develops autonomous surface vessels (ASVs) for surface missions. Underwater Division: Focuses on autonomous underwater vessels (AUVs) for tasks below the water surface. Barelang MRT - KRTI-VTOL: A new division specializing in Vertical Take-Off and Landing (VTOL) drones, expanding the team's expertise to aerial robotics. With these divisions, Barelang MRT continues to advance in marine, underwater, and aerial robotics at national and international levels.
The incorporation of these teams into Barelang MRT demonstrates the collaboration and integration of expertise in various fields of robotics, both marine and aerial.

National Competition

The Barelang MRT team also participated in several national competitions such as the National Unmanned Fast Boat Contest (KKCTBN), the Indonesian Robot Contest (KRI), and the Indonesian Flying Robot Contest (KRTI). For the National Unmanned Fast Boat Contest (KKCTBN) has the same concept as RoboBoat. for the Indonesian Robot Contest (KRI) is a robot contest that can move underwater. Vertical Take-Off and Landing (VTOL) Robot Contest, a flying robot capable of vertical take-off, stable flight, and able to carry out certain missions autonomously.

International Competition

The Barelang MRT team participated in several competitions such as RoboBoat which is an international competition held every year in Florida, USA. For this reason, the Barelang MRT team was formed to take part in its first competition, RoboBoat 2022. Until now, the Barelang MRT team has participated twice in international competitions start from RoboBoat 2022 to RoboBoat 2023.
